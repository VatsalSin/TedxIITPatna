Theme

Anwesha, IIT Patna has always been the ultimate abode of everything splendid and remarkable. It has always had the pleasure to impart the choicest of experiences and the best of encounters to everyone that is even remotely involved with it. Having completed 10 glorious volumes of brilliance, Anwesha unfurls it's eleventh edition as a tribute to the memories that we remember with a meek smile, the memories that remind us of all the good times, the memories we treasure that give us the much needed push to survive when we hit the ebb, the memories that have stayed ineradicably in our hearts and minds. Anwesha'20 is here to evoke all the past cherished memories and in the process, create many more new ones to be harboured for a lifetime to come.

Proudly unleashing, the theme of Anwesha 2020, A Reflection of Fond Remembrances.
This chapter, more colossal and grander than ever, all set to mark a permanent place in the scrapbook of the beautiful journey that life is.
